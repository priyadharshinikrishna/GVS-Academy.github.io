What We teach

BHARATHAM

Bharatanatyam is one of the oldest and most graceful classical dance forms of India, which originated in the temples of Tamil Nadu.

VOCAL

Vocal singing is the art of expressing emotions and thoughts through the human voice. It is one of the oldest and most natural forms of music.

VIOLIN

violin, bowed stringed musical instrument that evolved during the Renaissance from earlier bowed instruments.

VEENA

The veena is a revered, ancient Indian musical instrument, a stringed instrument deeply rooted in Indian classical music.

FLUTE

The flute is a woodwind instrument in which sound is produced by blowing air across a sharp edge, creating a vibrating column of air.

KEYBOARD

A keyboard is an input device that lets you type letters, numbers, and symbols into a computer or other electronic device.

MRITHANGAM

The mridangam is a double-headed, barrel-shaped drum, a primary percussion instrument in the Carnatic music tradition of South India.

GUITAR

The guitar is a popular, plucked-string musical instrument, typically with six strings, a fretted fingerboard, and a soundbox.
